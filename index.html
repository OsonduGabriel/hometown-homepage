Visit Buea

Explore the charm, culture, and natural beauty of Buea, the historic town at the foot of Mount Cameroon

Top six activities to do at Buea

Hike Mount Cameroon

Challenge yourslef with a trek up the highest mountain in West Africa. Enjoy breathtaking views and a unique adventure.

Explore Colonial Landmarks

Visit the German colonial buildings, the Prime Minister's Lodge, and other historical sites that tell the story of Buea's past.

Catch a local theater show

Experience live performaces at the heart of the city. our local theater features drama, comedy, and musical shows

Tour the University of Buea

Take a walk through Cameroon's first Anglo-saxon university and enjooy its vibrant srudent atmosphere and scenic environment.

Visit Buea Stadium

Watch local sports events or take a tour of this iconic Stadium that hosts regional matches and community gatherings.

Experience Cultural Activities

Join in traditional dances, festivals and storytelling events that showcase the rich cultural heritage of Buea and its people.
